# Awesome Spatial Omics Niche

A curated guide to spatial niche analysis — organized by how each method defines "niche," not by publication date.

This site is the companion to the [Awesome Spatial Omics Niche](https://github.com/LiudengZhang/awesome-spatial-omics-niche) list. While the awesome list provides a curated index of the best resources, this site goes deeper with concept explanations, paper analyses, method comparisons, and practical guides.

## The Core Question

Every spatial niche method makes an implicit choice: what does "niche" mean? A composition-based method counts neighboring cell types. An expression method averages neighboring transcriptomes. A communication method detects active signaling. These are genuinely different biological lenses, and the right choice depends on your question.

This site makes those implicit choices explicit.
